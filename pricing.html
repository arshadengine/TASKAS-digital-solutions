CLEAR ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you calculate custom project timelines?

Our project timelines are calculated based on wireframe approvals, feature complexity, and asset readiness. Typically, a Launch plan project takes 10-14 business days, a Growth plan platform takes 4-6 weeks, and Scale enterprise projects are scheduled in milestones with bi-weekly updates.

What is the payment structure?

For one-time projects, we work on a standard 50% upfront deposit and 50% upon project handoff and sign-off. For monthly retainer subscriptions, invoices are generated on the 1st of every month and cover unlimited revisions, continuous deployment, and server maintenance.

Can we switch or upgrade our billing plan later?

Absolutely. You can migrate from a one-time project to a monthly maintenance retainer or upgrade your tier mid-project. We'll simply calculate the pro-rated difference and adjust your upcoming milestone payments accordingly.

Who owns the code and final assets?

You do. Upon receipt of the final project milestone payment, full intellectual property rights, database schemas, codebase access (GitHub/GitLab repositories), and vector asset rights are entirely transferred to your company.
